    Alda Kote is a visual artist from Berat, Albania, raised in Thessaloniki, Greece, and now based in The Hague, The Netherlands.

    For more than a decade, photography has been her primary medium, a way to capture the quiet intensity of everyday life, the subtle shift of seasons, and the layered complexity of human identity. Her work moves fluidly between landscapes that evoke atmosphere and memory, and portraits that mirror the inner world of the psyche.

    In recent years, Alda has expanded her practice into graphic design, merging typography and imagery to craft visual pieces inspired by lyrics, films, and the textures of daily life. Alongside her artistic projects, she has collaborated on commercial and fashion photography, supporting small businesses and emerging creatives in bringing their visions to life.

    Beyond the lens, Alda has spent the last six years shaping content strategies and social media presences, leading rebrands, nurturing online communities, and launching creative campaigns that connect stories with audiences.
